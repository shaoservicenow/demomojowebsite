Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

DemoMojo is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service:

  • Extension Usage Data: Basic analytics about feature usage to improve our product
  • Account Information: Email address and basic profile information for Pro/Team accounts
  • Demo Content: Your demo stories, personas, and chapter data (stored locally by default)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our browser extension service
  • Process payments for Pro and Team subscriptions
  • Send important service updates and notifications
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data security is our priority:

  • Local Storage: Free tier data is stored locally in your browser
  • Cloud Storage: Pro/Team data is encrypted and stored securely
  • Encryption: All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption
  • Access Control: Limited access to your data by authorized personnel only

4. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services:

  • Payment Processing: Stripe for secure payment handling
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics to improve our service
  • Hosting: Secure cloud infrastructure for data storage

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your demo content
  • Opt out of marketing communications

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Authentication and session management
  • Remembering your preferences
  • Basic analytics (anonymized)

7. Children's Privacy

DemoMojo is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We'll notify users of significant changes via email or through the extension.
